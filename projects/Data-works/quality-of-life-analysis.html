üéØ Project Overview

Dive into the quality of life across various states with this detailed analysis. This project evaluates the quality of life indices for different states in 2024, focusing on overall scores and specific factors such as affordability, economy, education, and safety.

The analysis aims to uncover insights into the overall quality of life and its influencing factors, providing a comprehensive view of what makes certain states more livable than others.

üìä Dataset Description

The dataset comprises 50 rows (all US states) and 7 columns measuring different quality of life dimensions:

  • State: The state of residence
  • QualityOfLifeTotalScore: Overall quality of life score combining all factors
  • QualityOfLife: General quality of life assessment
  • QualityOfLifeAffordability: Cost of living and affordability score
  • QualityOfLifeEconomy: Economic conditions and opportunities
  • QualityOfLifeEducationAndHealth: Education system and healthcare quality
  • QualityOfLifeSafety: Safety, security, and crime rates

Data Quality

Perfect dataset: No missing values, no duplicates. Complete coverage of all 50 US states with comprehensive quality metrics.

üéâ Key Insights

  • Regional Patterns: Clear geographic clustering in quality of life scores, with certain regions consistently outperforming others
  • Affordability Trade-offs: High-scoring states often face affordability challenges, showing inverse relationship between opportunity and cost
  • Economic Impact: Strong economy scores correlate positively with overall quality of life, but don't guarantee high rankings alone
  • Education-Health Link: States excelling in education typically also show strong healthcare systems, suggesting interconnected social infrastructure
  • Safety Premium: Safety scores significantly influence overall quality rankings, especially for top-tier states
  • Balance Matters: Highest-ranked states achieve balance across all dimensions rather than excelling in just one area
