üéØ Project Overview

This project delves into the dynamic world of smartphones through exploratory data analysis (EDA). By examining a dataset with various attributes such as brand, model, price, specifications, and features, we aim to uncover trends and insights that drive the smartphone market.

The analysis explores 937 smartphones with 26 different attributes including brand names, specifications, pricing, ratings, and advanced features like 5G connectivity, NFC, processor details, camera specifications, and more.

üìä Dataset Description

The dataset comprises 937 rows and 26 columns with comprehensive smartphone attributes:

  • Brand & Model: Manufacturer and model identification
  • Pricing: Market prices across different segments
  • Connectivity: 5G, NFC, and IR Blaster capabilities
  • Processor: Brand, cores, and speed specifications
  • Battery: Capacity and fast charging support
  • Memory: RAM and internal storage specifications
  • Display: Screen size, resolution, and refresh rate
  • Cameras: Front and rear camera specifications
  • Operating System: Software platform information

  • Brand Dominance: Clear market leaders emerge in both budget and premium segments
